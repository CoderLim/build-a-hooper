## Get in Touch

We read every message sent to the addresses below. Whether you found a bug, have a leaderboard question, or want to suggest a feature, we would like to hear from you.

## Email

**General support and feedback**

[support@buildahooper.org](mailto:support@buildahooper.org)

Use this address for:

- Bug reports and broken pages
- Leaderboard or account issues
- Content corrections for guides and blog posts
- Privacy or terms questions
- General feedback about the site

## What to Include

To help us respond faster, please include:

- A short description of the issue or idea
- The page or feature involved (for example, leaderboard, game, blog)
- Your browser and device, if reporting a technical problem
- Screenshots or steps to reproduce, when relevant

## Response Time

We aim to reply within **2–3 business days**. Complex leaderboard or account investigations may take longer, but we will acknowledge receipt when possible.

## Community Links

## Privacy

Please do not send passwords or sensitive personal information by email. For details on how we handle information, see our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy).
