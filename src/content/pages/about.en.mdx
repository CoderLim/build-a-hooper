## What This Site Is

`buildahooper.org` is an unofficial community portal for **Build a Hooper**, a browser basketball simulator where you draft skills, tune attributes, explore game modes, and push through an 82-game season.

We built this site to make the game easier to discover, understand, and revisit. That means:

- A localized entry point with guides and FAQs
- A blog for strategy notes and site updates
- Leaderboards and profiles for players who want to compare runs
- Links to the public game hosted at `https://build-a-hooper.pages.dev/`

## Relationship to the Original Game

Build a Hooper is a fan-made browser game created independently of this portal. `buildahooper.org` is **not** the official homepage of the game and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the original creator unless explicitly stated otherwise.

Our role is to provide context, community features, and long-form content around the experience — not to replace the game itself.

## What We Are Building Toward

The long-term goal is simple: help players move from "I clicked through a few games" to "I understand how builds, modes, and season strategy fit together."

That includes:

- Clear onboarding through the homepage guide and FAQ
- Practical strategy writing in the blog
- Competitive comparison through leaderboards
- A Chrome extension that makes returning to the game faster

## Open Source and Extensions

Parts of the surrounding tooling are public. You can find the Chrome extension project on [GitHub](https://github.com/CoderLim/build-a-hooper-extension) or install it from the [Chrome Web Store](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/build-a-hooper-launcher/dccnpagebnanngljphnfbbfefnmhlckm).
