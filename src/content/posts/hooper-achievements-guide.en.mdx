## What Achievements track

Open [/achievements](/achievements) in the **hooper basketball game** for **52** goals under **Legacy Builder**. The catalog in the **hooper basketball game** falls into eight buckets:

- **Modes** — finish Classic / Blind / Chaos, then win each mode’s title
- **Competitive titles** — Chaos + Blind rings at 5, 11, 25, 50
- **Positions** — win as PG, SG, SF, PF, or C; all five = Complete Hooper
- **Rookies** — stack rookies, win with them, or go full all-rookie
- **Underdog** — Chaos/Blind at A- or lower, with or without a ring
- **Builds** — sniper, wall, goat blueprint, and similar shapes
- **Awards** — Scoring Title, MVP, DPOY, Finals MVP, and same-run combos
- **Legacy** — triple-doubles, 60/70/73 wins, Brooklyn, first banner, play-in miracle, 3-1 Finals comeback

Rarity runs common (10 pts) through Special (200) up to **GOAT** (300 pts). Full board is **4200** points — a long chase if you treat the **hooper basketball game** as a career, not a night.

## How unlocks work in the hooper basketball game

Nothing pops mid-sim. A season ends on **My Card**. Logged in, the run saves. Achievements recompute from your career when you open the page in the **hooper basketball game**.

1. Play the **hooper basketball game** in [/game](/game)
2. Finish and submit while signed in
3. Open [/achievements](/achievements)

Guests browse the catalog in the **hooper basketball game**. Bars and dates fill after login — same guest-first split Build a Hooper uses for Legacy.

Several ring ladders only count **Chaos and Blind**. Classic still fills mode, position, and build badges; Dynasty and Complete Hooper lean harder. Farm Classic only and big chunks stay locked — Chaos/Blind ring ladders, Complete Hooper, underdogs, Future GOATs. Intentional design in the **hooper basketball game**, not a bug.

## Modes, rings, positions

Modes are how the **hooper basketball game** changes what you see at draft. Classic: pick position, see grades, three rerolls. Blind: position shown, grades hidden. Chaos: both hidden until the end. That triad is the spine of the **hooper basketball game**; every mode badge maps back to it.

First-finish badges are easy. Mode rings sit one tier up. Competitive Titles count Chaos/Blind championships toward Ring Collector, Eleven Rings, Dynasty Builder, and Immortal Legacy — the grind lane once you are deep into the **hooper basketball game**.

Position rings work in any mode. Complete Hooper needs Chaos or Blind titles at all five spots. That rule keeps one autopilot build from clearing the board in the **hooper basketball game**.

## Builds, rookies, underdogs

Build goals read the 13 attributes you lock. Typical targets in the **hooper basketball game**:

- Sniper Build — 3PT and MID both A or better
- Floor General — PAS and HAN both A or better
- Defensive Wall — PDEF / IDEF / BLK / REB all B+ or better
- Perfect Balance — every attribute A or better
- GOAT Blueprint — overall A+ (97+)

Rookie badges count “lowest three on each source roster” rookies in your slots. Trust the Kid is one. All-Rookie Build wants the whole sheet. Future GOATs needs Chaos/Blind, all rookies, and a title — one of the louder flexes on the **hooper basketball game** board.

Underdog Run and Underdog Miracle sit on Chaos or Blind at overall A- or worse. Winning that hits different than A+ spam, which is why underdog goals exist in the **hooper basketball game** at all.

## Awards and legacy records

The **hooper basketball game** maps season awards to Scoring King, Season MVP, Defensive Anchor, and Finals MVP. Same-run combos unlock Complete Superstar, Offensive Takeover, Two-Way Monster, and Perfect Resume. Those lines sit next to the ring chase in the **hooper basketball game**.
